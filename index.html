About CASE Consultants

Leaders in innovative, sustainable, and cost-effective engineering solutions.

CASE Consultants is a structural engineering consultancy specializing in seismic resilience, seismic risk assessment, retrofit strategies, and performance-based design. CASE combines advanced engineering analysis with practical implementation for complex buildings and infrastructure systems.

CASE provides comprehensive services for multi-hazard scenarios, including earthquake, fire, and blast loading. The firm emphasizes robust, resilient, and cost-effective solutions for critical facilities, urban infrastructure, and high-consequence projects.
